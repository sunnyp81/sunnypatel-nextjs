These are AI Mode/Overview citation impressions: visibility without clicks. Response: maximise citability (answer-first blocks, self-contained claims, named entity) and accept the click comes later via brand/GBP.

4. **The technical-audit cluster is the single biggest clean opportunity:** ~10k impressions across "technical seo audit service/services/uk/price/consultant/consultancy/agency", ALL owned by /services/technical-seo-audit/ with zero cannibalisation, positions 15–58. "technical seo audit price" sits p15.2 (487 impr) with pricing intent and we now show £495.
5. Trailing-slash / legacy URL "duplicates" in GSC all 308 correctly (verified live 2026-07-19) — historical impressions, no action.
